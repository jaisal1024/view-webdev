Competition Information


Guidelines

Open to current NYU Abu Dhabi Students only

Each photo must include a short caption

Each photo must also be at least 10 Megabytes in size, therefore it must be taken on a digital camera

Submission will be judged anonymously by a panel of NYUAD Visual Arts faculty, local professional photographers, NYUAD Student Life stakeholders, and View Board members.

Timeline

Launch: Nov 14 -- Submission Form Opens & Website Live

Deadline: Dec 15 -- Submissions Form & Website Close

Winners Announced: Jan 10 -- Will be contacted by Email

Prizes

First Place : 500 AED

Second Place : 300 AED

Third Place : 100 AED

Honorable Mention : 50 AED

Winners photos will be used around campus by Student Life and View

Life doesn't wait on you

You may submit up to 5 photos. By submitting your photos, you are giving Student Life and View full creative and printing license
